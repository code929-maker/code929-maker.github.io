Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our networking services, virtual rooms, and professional connections.

How do I schedule a networking session? +

Simply browse our professional directory, select the person you'd like to connect with, and book a time slot that works for both of you through our AI-powered integrated calendar system.

Are virtual networking rooms available? +

Yes! Our immersive virtual networking rooms are available 24/7, allowing you to connect with professionals from around the world at your convenience using cutting-edge VR technology.

What's the difference between networking and mentoring? +

Networking focuses on building mutual professional relationships, while mentoring involves one-on-one guidance from experienced professionals to help advance your career through personalized AI-assisted matching.

Do I need a subscription to access services? +

We offer both free and premium membership tiers. Basic networking features are free, while advanced features like priority booking, AI recommendations, and exclusive events require a premium subscription.

Can I switch networking groups if I don't find the right fit? +

You can join multiple networking groups and switch between them based on your interests and professional goals. Our AI system will recommend the best groups for your profile.

Is my information and networking history kept confidential? +

Yes, we maintain strict confidentiality standards with enterprise-grade encryption. Your personal information and networking activities are protected and only shared with your explicit consent.
