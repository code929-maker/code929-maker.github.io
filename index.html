Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our networking services, virtual rooms, and professional connections.

How do I schedule a networking session? +

Simply browse our professional directory, select the person you'd like to connect with, and book a time slot that works for both of you through our integrated calendar system.

Are virtual networking rooms available? +

Yes! Our virtual networking rooms are available 24/7, allowing you to connect with professionals from around the world at your convenience.

What's the difference between networking and mentoring? +

Networking focuses on building mutual professional relationships, while mentoring involves one-on-one guidance from experienced professionals to help advance your career.

Do I need a subscription to access services? +

We offer both free and premium membership tiers. Basic networking features are free, while advanced features like priority booking and exclusive events require a premium subscription.

Can I switch networking groups if I don't find the right fit? +

You can join multiple networking groups and switch between them based on your interests and professional goals.

Is my information and networking history kept confidential? +

Yes, we maintain strict confidentiality standards. Your personal information and networking activities are protected and only shared with your explicit consent.
